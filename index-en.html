About Donglee Group

Founded in 2004 and headquartered in Guangzhou, China, Donglee is a national high-tech enterprise specializing in the research, development, and production of foot health products. We are committed to providing high-quality OEM and ODM services to the global market. Through advanced technological research and intelligent manufacturing, we have earned wide recognition and praise from both domestic and international customers. The company holds 27 patents and has introduced advanced intelligent automated production lines and management systems, significantly improving production efficiency and product quality. We have also obtained several international certifications, including ISO 13485, ISO 9001, GMP, and SEDEX.
